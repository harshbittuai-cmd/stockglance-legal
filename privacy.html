1. Information we collect

Stock-Glance collects only what it needs to run your watchlist and alerts:

Account information

Your email address, used to sign in. Your password is handled entirely by our authentication provider (Supabase) — Stock-Glance never sees or stores your raw password.

Data you create in the app

The stocks you track, the price alerts you set, any investment notes you write, and any industry trackers you build. This is your data, stored so it syncs across your sessions.

Push notification token

A device identifier used only to deliver the price alert notifications you set up.

Usage analytics

We use PostHog to understand how the app is used — which screens are opened and general app activity — associated with your account so we can debug issues and improve the app.

2. How we use it

  • To sync your watchlist, alerts, and notes across your sessions and devices.
  • To check your price alerts against live market data and send you a push notification when one triggers.
  • To generate AI news briefings for stocks you're watching.
  • To understand app usage and fix problems.

We do not sell your data. We do not show ads, and we do not use your information for cross-app or cross-site ad tracking.

3. Third-party services

Stock-Glance relies on a small number of service providers to function. Stock data and news requests are routed through our own servers, so these providers generally only see the stock tickers involved, not who you are.

4. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists.

You can permanently delete your account and everything associated with it — watchlist, alerts, notes, and industry trackers — at any time, directly in the app: Settings → Delete Account. This takes effect immediately and cannot be undone. You don't need to contact us to do this.

5. Security

Your data is protected by database-level access rules that ensure only you can read or modify your own watchlist, alerts, and notes — not even our own backend code can bypass this for routine requests. All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit.
